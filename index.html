Instructional Design | EdTech | E-Learning

I’m a proficient instructional designer, e-learning specialist, and educator in math and computer science. I create engaging, interactive learning experiences using Articulate Storyline, Moodle, H5P, Synthesia, Figma, Canva, and more. With a passion for innovative EdTech solutions, I design courses that are both effective and visually compelling.

Work directions

E-Learning Development & Multimedia Design

Crafting interactive modules, quizzes, and simulations to bring learning to life. Leveraging Articulate Storyline, Figma, Canva, and Synthesia, I create multimedia-rich content that enhances engagement. My work also includes producing video-based learning materials and AI-powered training solutions, making education more dynamic and immersive.

Instructional Design & Curriculum Development

Designing engaging and pedagogically sound courses tailored for diverse learners. Using ADDIE, Bloom’s Taxonomy, and other instructional design models, I create structured content that enhances learning outcomes. My expertise includes developing interactive experiences with Moodle, H5P, and Articulate Storyline to ensure engaging and effective learning.

Learning Management System (LMS) Implementation & Optimization

Setting up and managing Moodle and other LMS platforms to provide seamless learning experiences. I specialize in customizing courses with gamification, analytics, and adaptive learning features, ensuring learners stay engaged. With a focus on usability, I optimize LMS functionality for smooth navigation and mobile compatibility.

Mathematics & Computer Science Education

Designing interactive STEM, math, and programming courses that foster critical thinking. My approach includes developing problem-solving exercises and hands-on coding tutorials to support skill development. By integrating cutting-edge EdTech tools, I create learning experiences that boost comprehension and engagement.
