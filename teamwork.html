Make the teamwork great again!

"Make the teamwork great again" is an e-learning course that aims to teach students the basic competencies related to teamwork and team building. The course can be deemed a personal aid for the students, assisting them in learning how to encounter and address the issues that can occur in any team project. The course is structured as a story that is related to the real situation — conducting the study team project for the Statistics class.

Features

The key features include a realistic main cover story, which involves the learner and gives him a role as a part of the team. The learner can influence the direction of the story through interactive solving. The immediate feedback allows the students to understand the relationship between their decisions and new knowledge, they learn from the story.

Additionally, some parts of the story, followed by the videos, relate to the learning goal that is implemented into the story fragment.

Characters

In our case, there are 5 students (Aspen Apathos, Elmer Kakashka, Mia Carey, Dex-ter Pointexter, and Axel Savage) who were first sketched and later designed to be so different from each other and represent various characteristics, which can be encoun-tered in any project group in real life.

Branched Scenarios

The learner definitely has a role as a member of the team. He is included in the story, and his answers influence the direction of the story. The scenario-related actions bring together all the features: the actions of the learner, a mission, and the learning objectives. There are specific actions of the learner in our course—to face the problematic situation and choose the best solution for the scenario. Alongside, the learner gets feedback from which he or she corrects mistakes, acquires new knowledge, or gets confirmation of the decision.
